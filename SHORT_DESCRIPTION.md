# AI Personal Safety Guardian - Short Description

**AI Personal Safety Guardian** is an intelligent safety analysis platform powered by Google Gemini AI that helps users assess potential threats in real-time through multiple input modalities. The application analyzes text descriptions, images, videos, and audio recordings to detect safety risks and provides actionable recommendations. Additionally, it offers location-based safe route guidance to help users navigate to secure destinations.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Modal Threat Analysis**: Analyze text, images, videos, and audio for potential safety threats
- **Real-Time Safety Assessment**: Get instant threat level ratings (Low, Medium, High, Critical) with detailed explanations
- **Safe Route Guidance**: Share your location to receive AI-powered recommendations for the safest nearby destinations
- **Emergency Quick Access**: Quick access to emergency contacts and safety guidelines
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Clean, intuitive design with interactive chat-based interface

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **AI Engine**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash API
- **File Handling**: Multer for media uploads

## Use Cases

- Analyzing suspicious situations described in text
- Assessing safety of locations through uploaded images
- Detecting threats in video recordings
- Analyzing audio for distress signals or dangerous sounds
- Finding safe routes when feeling unsafe in an area
